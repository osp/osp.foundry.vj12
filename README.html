Ubiquitous Grey literature radiates out from the margins and regulates the relationship between machines and humans.

A font developed for the 2009 edition of the Verbindingen/Jonctions Festival. The images on the festival’s posters were text files. A font may be a set of black blocks!

It is based on Linux Libertine. Once the measurements have been made, these instructions apply to any glyph:
pen.moveTo((left,desc))
pen.lineTo((left,asc))
pen.lineTo((right,asc))
pen.lineTo((right,desc))
pen.closePath()
